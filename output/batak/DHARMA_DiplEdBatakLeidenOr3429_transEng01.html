    1.

    Introduction

    1

    Teaching/Instruction of the core of the mountain-oracle to be observed either during the ceremony horja Sita-sita, or people make us into datu during a great war, or people make us into datu in inaugurating a new settlement, or we are celebrating a marriage, or we are offering unhusked rice on the rice field for the feast-giving group, or we are bringing our child to the river, or during the porsahan ceremony of the man who died old, or during the magic warfare.
