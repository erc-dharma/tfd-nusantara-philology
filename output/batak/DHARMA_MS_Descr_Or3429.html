  • Physical Description: Tree-bark 27 folios 27 24 The texts run from folio A1 to A27, and from B1 to B26, B27 is left blank. The beginning of a text is precedeed by the bindu na godang whereas the division into smaller sections of the texts is stresssed by the bindu na metmet . From from B1 to B9 the internal division of the text are stressed by another type of bindu na metmet in which its upper right-side has been elongated shaping a single black line . The black line preceedes and runs parallel to the first line of the text, until the right-side margin. On these same folios, the each small section of text is accompanied by a drawing, rapresenting different antrophomorphic figures.
