Description of the witness

  • Manuscript Identity:
    • Settlement: London
    • Institution: Royal Asiatic Society
    • Repository: Manuscripts
    • Identifier: Raffles Java 1
  • Physical Description:
    • Hand Description: A1H1

      Main hand of the manuscript, black ink, an apparently rather successful eye-copy of Old West Javanese Quadratic Script. The layar is noted in Indonesian mode.

    • History: Acquired by Stamford Raffles from an as yet undetermined source.
