chapter 1: 
    kiraṇo vyaktiś candrāgneḥ,
  dvijānāṁ kavir uttamaḥ.[1.1]
 
 [1.1] nahan byakta pinakaśarīra bhaṭāra, milvājanmāgave bhuvana, sira tāṣṭatanu ṅaranira, sira  tāṣṭadeśomidər    pinakavəṅku, sira ta huripnikaṅ rāt,    sira dumadyakən vr̥ddhiniṅ janma kabeh, prāṇaprāṇī sarva hurip,  ikanaṅ hurip lavan avak, śarīra pamisanya riṅ sādhārādheya, gumavay ikaṅ sarva dadi, sira sinaṅguh pūrvaniṅ janma. 
 [1.2] umijil taṅ aṣṭatanu  (riṅ) rat, nara, paśu, mr̥ga, janma, sthāvara, pakṣi, sarīsr̥pa, mīna, prabhāvaniṅ   ādhārādheya. 
  [1.3] prabhu rāma r̥ṣi, tiga sira, pramāṇa riṅ rat, pakə̄niṅ bhuvana, tuvi sira tunduk apeśīki, guruniṅ janma kabeh, lvirnira, irikā ta chanda ṅaranira, ṅkāna sira tika siddhaguru, sira tāṣṭagaṇa guṇanira gumavay ikaṅ varta sək lumrā. 
  
chapter 2: 
   ikaṅ akṣaropamāvak,
 ika ta guru ṅaranya,
 yeki ta vinastu hurip,
 ya sinaṅguh guru laghu.[2.1]

  ya pinakamūla chanda,
 avaknikaṅ guru laghu,
 lavan hurip masaṅyoga,
 umijil tikaṅ trigaṇa.[2.2]

  adəgnikaṅ guru laghu,
 aniga-nigeṅ sagaṇa,
 yekāṣṭagaṇa ṅaranya,
 sama tābyāpāreṅ vr̥tta.[2.3]

  mvaṅ laku śloka nihan len.[2.4]

   
chapter 3: 
    sakveh saṅ sujanātiharṣa irikaṅ chandā nihan kavruhi,
 yan mahyun vruha rūpaniṅ laghu lavan tekā sinaṅguh guru,
 bhedanyaṅ guru lāghu yeṅətakəna mvaṅ huṅgvaniṅ hakṣara ,
 sakvehniṅ guru yeriṅən hila-hilekā yan salah kasthānan.[3.1]

   byaktanyan guru sarva dīrgha taruṅā saṅyogapārānəṅən,
 lāvan karṇa visarja kāni surahan svārāṅ vatək vyañjana,
 e ai nāhan anuṅ kayatnakəna len o au vəkasniṅ guru,
 vus meṅət pva kiterikā niyata yan sotanta dadyā vacan.[3.2]

  lvirniṅ hakṣara gantuṅən mapakəneṅ vaktā mvaṅ aṅgantuṅa,
 saṅyogāpara sūkṣma lakṣmaṇa śivāgni kleśa lakṣmi kunaṅ,
 ndan glāna sthula nagna vighna maməvə̄ nirvighna māra sthiti,
 svapna sneha rikaṅ dvitīya kahaḍaṅ riṅ dlāha yan pāphala.[3.3]

    vahniṅ tīkṣṇa murub riṅ adri ya katon raśminya sāteja ya,
 masnāna pva kiteṅ nadī masəh ikaṅ saśleṣma kumlābakən,
 riṅ smāroṅgvasahiṣṇu nora pinaṅan maṅlih mvaṅ aṅgantuṅa,
 yapvan bheda sakojarojarakənan maṅke baliknyān vacan.[3.4]

       gəlana saṅ lagənān para riṅ səma,
 vighəna tīkṣəṇa yva giritulya ya,
 divadaśeka tahun yaśa nirghana,
 masupəna pva vadhū suməneha ya.[3.5]
  
  vagəmi yan  paḍəmiṅ bahənī hati,
 tuvi kəleśa vināśa salakṣəmi,
 səpinikaṅ bratamārganikān kələm,
 kramaniṅ akṣara yan jinajār ika.[3.6]

     lvirniṅ saṅyogaparekī,
 kakyan-kakyan kakvan-kakvan,
 kakran anəṅən aṅgantuṅ,
 kayatnakən təmən-təmən.[3.7]

          anta pada guru yati,
 kalavan svara kamadhya,
 dīrghādīrghavr̥tta gati,
 tāvat guru prasiddha ya.[3.8]

            dīrghātaruṅ asurahan,
 visarga lavan ajuṅjuṅ,
 kalavan guru saṅyoga,
 nahan taṅ sinaṅguh guru.[3.9]

       nihan taṅ svara kamadhya,
 a ā i ī u ū e o,
 tan dadi muṅguh riṅ təṅah,
 vvītan juga prakr̥tinya.[3.10]

       
chapter 4: 
    sampun ikaṅ pratekanikanaṅ guru dinəliṅakən,
 nyāt ajarən muvah paduluriṅ guru laghu  vuvusən,
 kvehnya savarga muṅguh ikanaṅ vacan aniga-niga,
 yeka sinaṅguhan gaṇa ṅaranya sipatiṅ aṅavi.[4.1]

  pūrva nihan makāra ṅaraniṅ guru tiga sagaṇa,
 lāghu kunaṅ panəṇḍasani luṅguhi yakaragaṇa,
 yapvan ikaṅ rakāragaṇa lāghu maḍuḍuk i təṅah,
 hetunikaṅ sakāragaṇa matvaṅ aguru vəkasan.[4.2]

    vruh pva kitā takāra ṅaraniṅ laghu təpi kavuri,
 jātinikaṅ jakāragaṇa maṅkana guru ri təṅah,
 bhakti jugaṅ bhakāragaṇa riṅ guru sira rumuhun,
 nākaragāṇa təlva madulur tika ya laghu paḍa.[4.3]

  nyaṅ laghu karva saṅ guru pasəṅgahanika si la ga,
 śeṣanikā gaṇeki vəkasiṅ guru laghu vinuvus,
 riṅ haji chanda teki yan avās śiva haji piturun,
 saṅ r̥ṣi vaṅśapatrapatitā sira tiki manatha.[4.4]

     
chapter 5: 
    nihan gaṇaniṅ ārya yeṅətakəna,
 jakāra kalavan bhakāra sakara,
 catur laghu guru rva yeka ta lima,
 ləvihnya guru lāghu kānəm ikahən.[5.1]

  lavan hana ta mātra mātra yeṅətakəna,
 vilaṅnya rikanaṅ sapāda matətəg,
 ikaṅ pada kapūrva kātəlu kunaṅ,
 gənəp rvavəlas aṅgəpanya tuturən.[5.2]

  kapiṅ rvanikihən pva ya vvaluvəlas,
 tumut limavəlas padanya vəkasan,
 tan evəha ya tā pat ārya tāpa tārya vinuvus,
 samaṅkana vilaṅni mātranikihən.[5.3]

    mvaṅ uṅgvanikanaṅ jakāra matətəg,
 kapiṅ rva maṅənəm dvitīya kasulam,
 pada rva ya ta hīṅaniṅ vvaṅ amilaṅ,
 kayatnakəna saṅ vatək kavivara.[5.4]

    
chapter 6: 
    trigaṇa guṇāgaṇita ya guṇitan,
 prakaraṇa kīraṇa vaṇa karuṇa,
 aruṇa darūṇa varuṇa śaraṇa,
 avaraṇa bhāraṇa ṅ apaṇa ṇa gə̄ṅ.[6.1]

      guṇa kuṇi kuṇa kaṇṭi kaṇika-ṇika len,
 ghraṇa ṅ agaṇa-gaṇa grahaṇa samiraṇa,
 praṇava vaṇiya pāṇiya maṇita-ṇitan,
 ayaṇa karuṇa pāṇa ṇa gəṅa yatika.[6.2]

        paṇḍita pāṇḍava pāṇḍura pāṇḍu,
 piṇḍa ṅ apiṇḍa mapaṇḍəṅ amaṇḍi,
 laṇḍap alaṇḍəp alaṇḍuṅ aləṇḍə̄,
 gaṇḍi-giṇaṇḍi diṇəṇḍa ṇa magəṅ.[6.3]

    dhāraṇa vāhaṇa vāraṇa bāṇa,
 veśraṇa varṇa maṇī śaṇa karṇa,
 arṇava kīrṇa suparṇa yavorṇā,
 cūrṇita pūrṇama ghūrṇita ṇāgəṅ.[6.4]

      kagantuṅa deniṅ ṇa gəṅ,
 ṭa ṭha ḍa ḍha ṇa riṅ ṇāguṅ,
 ta tha da dha na riṅ nālit,
 varṇādoh ya sa savarga.[6.5]

  liṅ saṅ kavi vruheṅ mārga.[6.6]

          uṅgvanikaṅ na litāpakəneki,
 ndā savanehnya rikaṅ pakənanta,
 nāta samaṅkana teka na ḍə̄mit,
 kapva masaṅkhya nihan savanehnya.[6.7]
 
    
chapter 7: 
       muni muna nāna samanuṣa,
 anumana hana nami-nami,
 dina dhana nādi nada nana,
 hani hina tan hana inituṅ.[7.1]

      praṇata natha prajapātini tanu ya,
 tanah atunah pina tattva ta tanaya,
 nita vanitā nini-nīni tanu tani,
 manana vanā malinī na lit ikahən.[7.2]

     vaneh pakənaniṅ na lit,
 nāga punnāganiṅ gunuṅ,
 nāga nāgendra nāgendrī,
 nāgari nāga nāginī.[7.3]

  magəni yāgni yāgneya,
 gagana dinanān dina,
 ghana vāhana vājinī vāni,
 nītinitya yanuṅ na lit ika.[7.4]

  dahana jvalana vanā,
 kāminī vāhinī nini,
 punah vinā vani vana,
 vinavan i na lit ika.[7.5]

  saṅkṣepanya yan vuvusən,
 yāvat punah rakva rəṅva pūrva,
 pa ba mādi kunaṅ pūrva,
 dadi na lit dadi ṇa gəṅ.[7.6]

  na vyaktanyan huvus kojar,
 kekadeśanya kokta ya,
 kocapa teki śa ṣa sa, 
 byaktanya matra varṇanən.[7.7]

   
chapter 8: 
    śambhu śaṅkara śarā śaraṇa śaśī,
 deśa deśika śaśaṅ śavala kuśa,
 śvāśucī śuna śirah śiva dr̥śana,
 śānti śoka paśa śāstra śuna śiśu.[8.1]

      śa mūrdhanyātekaṅ śa kurəb umunīṅ vunvunikahən,
 śaśāṅkā śaṅkhā śaṅkara kuśa-kuśā keśi kuśika,
 kuśa śrota krośa kraniki katha śabdeṅ śikhariṇī,
 śikhi ṅ keśīkeśā praśa śakuni śakuni keśī kuśa-kuśa.[8.2]

    śanaih śūnya śreṇi ghrāṇa piśunane śrīyaśa niśā,
 vināśa mvaṅ māśā daśadiśi śarat śūra raśika,
 śivā śambhuh śūla triśula muśala śrī vava śiva,
 śavah vaṅśah veśyah śavari śivirākāśa śucimān.[8.3]

     
chapter 9: 
    kṣaṇa kṣiṇi kapakṣa lakṣmi viṣayā hahə̄man nidhi,
 kagantuṅa gumantuṅeṅ ṭa ḍa kunaṅ ṣa pr̥thvītala,
 prahr̥ṣṭi parituṣṭa roṣa ruṣa poṣya kr̥ṣṇa proṣa,
 akarṣaṇa ya vr̥ṣṇi vr̥ṣṭa ṣa lumah ṣa pr̥thvītala.[9.1]

    maharṣi mahiṣī mahoṣadha maheṣa meṣā muṣa,
 rəṣah muṣika puṣya poṣaka puṣa praṣaṣṭā maṣa,
 ṣaḍaṅguli ṣaḍaṅga bhūṣaṇa subhāṣa durbhāṣita,
 viṣān uṣadha yauṣadhīṣu viṣama dvipādāsana.[9.2]

    bhaviṣyati hiriṣya harṣaṇa puṣā uṣā tyāṣa len,
 viṣa ṅ viṣaya yomiśeṣa dadi mānuṣā kāṣaya,
 nyataṅ kaluṣa kilviṣa ṅ ayuṣa kaṣmalā doṣa ya,
 nyataṅ mami ṣa yoṅgvaniṅ ṣa marənah riṅ anta ta ya.[9.3]
 
       śākāśa mvaṅ sapr̥thvī sadulur ika rəṅən yekihən varṇanən ta,
 śuśrūṣā śiṣya śikṣā śviśita vətunikaṅ huśvāsa śeṣaśeṣan,
 śaśvat śīrṣanya śośvi śvaṣinaka śaśi de saṅ vasiṣṭhā śaviṣṭi,
 maṅkā tekaṅ śaṭhāvor ṣa ta yat iṅət-iṅət yeki sākṣāt vətunya.[9.4]

     
chapter 10: 
    māsānikaṅ sa ta nihan vuvusən padanya,
 sādhū sadā sata sabhā sisi tāsitāsi,
 sītā susatya rasa satriya sāri saṅ hyaṅ,
 svecchā svabhāva ri sarasvati sāri-sāri.[10.1]

   samvā savah vasumatī vasusena sūnu,
 mvaṅ ekavāsa ṅ upavāsa  vināsa-vāsa,
 durbhāṣa duryaśana durvyasana praśasta,
 vyāsā sasādhu sasivin sapujin sapūjan.[10.2]

   sasvāṅga saṅga sigi seṅgita sarga sajja,
 svaṅgah səga ṅ siga sugih sigaran sagāsor,
 sasvarga sāgara sagorava sādhaka syaṅ,
 rāktosvaniṅ sata sarat pakənanya sākṣāt.[10.3]

     sura sapta sarah surasa sira sadā,
 samasih papasən savara suta saha,
 savinaṅ savanən sakala sakavaśa,
 praja nāmi sa dantya niti kavivara.[10.4]

 [11.1] nyan sūtrasandhi vuvusən, parakr̥ta lvirnika patiṅkahnya, atambayan avəkasan, nāhan ta ya sinaṅguhan varṇa. 
 [11.2] tiniṅkah pinarva vəkasan, ikaṅ namah yekihən svara ṅaranya, yapvan kapurvakanta, sinaṅguhan vyañjana nāma. 
 [11.3] l̥, yan ta adi samāna, tumūt sinama, i pūrvaka, vəkasan ghoṣa, sahle sah ri svabatək, a i u r̥ l̥, lvirnya kavruhana. 
 [11.4] dīrgha ṅaranya manəhər, ā ī ū r̥̄ l̥̄, limaṅ viji sahāya, sandhyakṣara muvah aṅgəpan, e ai o au, nahan lvirnya. 
 [11.5] ka kha ca cha (ṭa ṭha) ta tha pa pha, sakatəlu nihan sahayaniṅ aghoṣa, ga gha ṭa ṭha ya ra la va ha, sighoṣa rakva nāmanika. 
 [11.6] varga ṅaranya limalikur, ka pinakapurvanikaṅ ma, ma vəkasan pinakavəkasnya, ka ga kalimanya ya śuddhan, ra śa ṣa sahayanya vr̥ddhivr̥ddha. 
 [11.7] anunāsika ṅa ña ṇa na ma, antaḥstha ṅaranikaṅ ya ra la va, tūt ūṣman śa ṣa sa ha saṁvidhā, patiṅkahniṅ sūtrasandhi ika. 
 
chapter 12: 
 [12.1] sampun pratyekanikanaṅ akṣara riṅ vyañjana lavan svara, t ucapa ta sañjatanikahən, prakarani ṅaranya kavruhana. 
 [12.2] ṅaranikaṅ hulu savaneh, makuṭa rukuh agra mastaka kapāla, tijakula śekhara maśirah, nahan ta parināmaniṅ śr̥ṅga. 
 [12.3] dīrgha hulu mapalenan, utək lavan kambara kuñcir, majaṭa, magumbak majambul aṅurai, svanāmani dīrgha yan yan riṅ hulu. 
 [12.4] vindu lavan madana kunaṅ, kunaṅ-kunaṅ len tāra lavan vintaṅ, tilaka titik kani surahan, nāhan ta parināmaniṅ canda. 
 [12.5] nyan ta ṅ cakra jantra guluṅan, sudarśanāyudha kr̥ṣṇa len śakaṭa, daśanāma yeki huniṅan, mvaṅ añakra hulu yukti kavruhana. yapvan ikaṅ taliṅa nihan, kuṇḍala sumpiṅ capiṅ len karṇa, śrota suvəṅ mvaṅ iṅət-iṅət, palaga sunāmaniṅ taliṅa. 
 [12.6] dīrgha ṅaranya, tulalai, iku ṅaran varāstra len galah adava, kirivili kuñca ya savaneh, asiṅ adava dīrgha rakveka. 
 [12.7] aṅrasuk agoduha keśa mavaju, kīrtti kunaṅ saṅka len kuruṅan, ikaṅ kalambi lavan hana manuṅgaṅ, matapu-tapuṅ hana mapayuṅ, ikaṅ sinaṅguh kalambi vaneh, daśanāmaniṅ taruṅ ṅaranya nahan. 
 [12.8] yapvan vatək makəṇḍit gaṇitri lavan goduha, vaja sahāroha, makiratbāhu, bhāmana jənu vaneh, visarjanīyeki nahan sabatək. 
 [12.9] mati pəjah śūnya turu kunaṅ, paratrakəna bajra līnāguliṅan, hanāpayuṅ marukuh len asoṅsoṅ, nāhan katəṅən ika, makādi vəkasniṅ yati iṅətakəna saṅ ajñānan. 
 [12.10] matəkən gadā suku-suku, palu-palu daṇḍa saha gada, vəntis alaris pva kaguritan, asiṅ adava jə̄ṅnya yeki kənakən. 
 [12.11] vatək təvək aluṅid mapañjaṅ mvaṅ paṇḍi, curiga gaḍiṅ candrahāsa suṅu tinyup, sihuṅ umiṅis, ya ta malaris curik nahan yukti kakyakyan. 
 [12.12] śivapattra ya ta tinampil, masidəkuṅ atvaṅ aməluk siṅhəl, muṅgv iṅ paṭāraṇa, bantal karaṅ hulu yayā ta kakvakvan. 
 [12.13] hana ta maṅhir vəḍihan aḍeṅ, kr̥tala bhujaga nāgapāśa pinutərnya, kadi vaṅkava ya lumaṅkuṅ, laras vinəṇṭaṅnya, ya ta kakrakran. 
 [12.14] aṅkus galahnya binala, kuku manol sagayur jña sivur, pahat ya tinavanya, nahan pasidəkuṅ aməluk tur, kukunya tumakul kadi kre-kre. 
 [12.15] hana tāṅiṇḍit kur lavak, hanan pamava gadā nāgakonta, tuhuk limpuṅ kr̥tala paṇḍi curik, salvirnikaṅ inuṇḍa, yayanyan ra jinuṅjuṅ ika.

chapter A: 
   saṅ hyaṅ jagattripuruṣa,
 namo śivāya ta ṅhulun,
 amvīt umastave kita,
 amarṇanā vr̥tta vətun.[A.1]

  guruniṅ jagat kita ya,
 mūlākṣara guru laghu,
 mātra āryā ya taṅ gaṇa,
 nāhan ṅaran tataṅ chanda.[A.2]

  kita makarūpa chanda,
 agave titi mvaṅ śloka,
 ya ta varṇanən kavətun,
 ya pinakasuluh iṅ rāt.[A.3]

  lvirniṅ chanda ta ṅaranya,
 kvehiṅ vilaṅniṅ akṣara,
 rikanaṅ pāda sayati,
 pāda ṅaranya sakaṇḍa,
 pataṅ pāda ya saśloka.[A.4]

  mūlanyān keṅətakəna,
 akṣara tuṅgal iṅ yati,
 yeka sinaṅguh antyanta,
 rvaṅ akṣara atyukta (ṅa).[A.5]

  tryakṣara madhyama nāma,
 pratiṣṭha pataṅ akṣara,
 śīrṣapratiṣṭha yan lima,
 nəmaṅ akṣara gāyatrī.[A.6]

   uṣṇik ri pituṅ akṣara,
 vvalu pva ya pādānuṣṭubh,
 vr̥hati saṅaṅ akṣara,
 sapuluh pva si paṅkti.[A.7]

  svasaṅvāda pva savəlas,
 rvavəlas pva ya jagatī,
 atijagatī tigavlas,
 śakvarī ya padblas ika.[A.8]

   atiśakvarī limavlas,
 aṣṭi nəmbəlas ikahən,
 atyaṣṭi nahan pituvlas,
 dhr̥ti yapvan vvaluvəlas.[A.9]

   atidhr̥ti saṅavəlas,
 rvaṅpuluh ya tānu kr̥ti,
 prakr̥ti ya ta salikur,
 rvalikur ikaṅ ākr̥ti.[A.10]

  təlulikur iṅ vikr̥ti,
 padlikur sunāmakr̥ti,
 abhikr̥ti limalikur,
 an utkr̥ti ya nəmlikur.[A.11]

    [.1] nahan pratiṅkahnya, sampun ika, samaṅkana vilaṅniṅ vr̥ttiniṅ chanda, yapvan ləvih sakeṅ nəmlikur,  rakva ṅaranya, lvih saṅke rika, kalalu ṅaranya, daṇḍaka i ruhurnya.
