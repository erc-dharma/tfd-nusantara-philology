Dyad 1

ehi vatsa mahāyānaṁ mantracaryānayaṁ vidhiṁ

deśayiṣyāmi te samyak bhājanas tvaṁ mahānaye ||1||


(1)

ka: saṅ hyaṅ mahāyāna iki varahakna mami iri kita, mantracaryānayaṁ vidhiṁ, saṅ hyaṅ mantranaya sira mahāyāna mahāmārga ṅaranira, deśayiṣyāmi te ⟨MS: 9r⟩ samyak, sira teki deśanākna mami varahakna mami ri kita, bhājanas tvaṁ mahānaye, ri kadadinyan kita pātrabhūta yogya varahən ri saṅ hyaṅ dharma mantranaya.


Dyad 2

atītā ye hi sambuddhāḥ tathā caivāpy anāgatāḥ

pratyutpannāś ca ye nāthāḥ tiṣṭhanti ca jagaddhitāḥ ||2||


ka: bhaṭāra hyaṅ buddha saṅ atīta, saṅ maṅabhisaṁbuddha ṅūni riṅ āsītkāla, kady aṅgān: bhaṭāra vipaśyī, viśvabhū, krakucchanda, kanakamuni, kāśyapa, atītabuddha, ṅaranira kabeh. tathā caivāpy anāgatāḥ, kunaṅ bhaṭāra buddha saṅ anāgata, saṅ abhimukha maṅabhisaṁbuddha, kady aṅgān: bhaṭāra āryamaitreyādi, samantabhadraparyanta, anāgatabuddha ṅaranira kabeh ⟨MS: b9⟩ pratyutpannāś ca ye nāthāḥ, tumamvah bhaṭāra śrī śākyamuni, vartamānabuddha ṅaranira, sira ta pinakahyaṅ buddhanta maṅke, śāsananira ikeṅ tinūt atinta. tiṣṭhanti ca jagaddhitāḥ, tamolah ta sira kumiṅkiṅ hitasukhaniṅ sarvasattva, umaṅənaṅəna kaləpasanikaṅ rāt kabeh saka riṅ saṅsāra, duvəg kumirakira paḍamaniṅ mahāpralaya rike bhuvana.

(2)(3)

Dyad 3

taiś ca sarvair imaṁ vajraṁ jñātvā mantravidhim paraṁ

prāptā sarvajñatā vīraiḥ bodhimūle hy alakṣaṇā ||3||


(4)

ka: sira katiga bhaṭāra hyaṅ buddha ṅaranira, saṅ atītānāgatavartamāna, tan hana mārganira vaneh ar tinambakan ikaṅ kahyaṅbuddhan. jñātvā mantravidhim paraṁ, ikiṅ >mahāyāna mahāmārga ya tinūtakənira, pinakamārganira ar ḍataṅ rikana nirvāṇanagara. ⟨MS: 10r⟩ prāptā sarvajñatā vīraiḥ bodhimūle hy alakṣaṇā, inakni denira guməgə̄ ikaṅ mantranaya, ya ta mataṅ yar təmvakan kasarvajñān, ya ta hetunirār paṅguhakan ikaṅ kahyaṅbuddhān riṅ bodhimūla.

(5)(6)(7)(8)

Dyad 4

mantraprayogam atulaṁ yena bhagnaṁ mahābalaṁ

mārasainyam mahāghoraṁ śākyasiṅhena tāyinā ||4||


ka: bhaṭāra śrī śākyamuni mataṅ yar təmvakan ikaṅ kamāravijayan, sakvehnikanaṅ māravighna alah denira: kleśamāra, skandhamāra, mr̥tyumāra, devaputramāra, alah aluyuk ikā kabeh de bhaṭāra hetunirār vənaṅ umalahakən ikaṅ māra, ābhānubhāva prabhāva saṅ hyaṅ samādhi śakti saṅ hyaṅ mantranaya inabhyāsa.

(9)

Dyad 5

tasmān matim imāṁ vatsa kuru sarvajñatāptaye

⟨MS: 10v⟩śr̥ṇu bhadrāśayaṁ nityaṁ samyak saṁhr̥tya kalpanāḥ ||5||


(10)(11)

ka: mataṅyan deyanta kuru sarvajñātāptaye, hayva tālaṅalaṅ aṅənaṅənta rike saṅ hyaṅ mantranaya, pahapagəh denta guməgə̄ saṅ hyaṅ mantranaya, mataṅ yan kapaṅguha ikaṅ kasarvajñatān denta; śr̥ṇu bhadrāśayaṁ nityaṁ, pahavās denta ruməṅə̄ iki varavarah mami ri kita, hayva tan yatna vuvus mami. samyak saṅhr̥tya kalpanāḥ, karyakan taṅ buddhi savikalpaka, hilaṅakan taṅ ambək abhiniveśa, pahenak tāṅənaṅənta, hayva saṅśaya.

(12)(13)(14)(15)(16)(17)

Dyad 6

eṣa mārgavaraḥ śrīmān mahāyānamahodayaḥ

yena yūyaṁ gamiṣyanto bhaviṣyatha tathāgatāḥ ||6||


ka: saṅ hyaṅ mahāyāna mahāmārga iki pintonakna mami ri kita, pahavās denta maṅrəṅə̄, mahāyānamahodayaḥ, ⟨MS: 11r⟩ yeki havan abənər təka ri svargāpavarga, vənaṅ umehakən ikaṅ kamahodayān. mahodaya, ṅa, ikaṅ bāhyādhyātmikasukha, vāhyasukha, ṅa, ikaṅ kaśreṣṭhyan, kasugihan, kapaməgətan, karatun, kacakravartin. ādhyātmikasukha, ṅa, ikeṅ lokottarasukha inak tan pavor duhkha, ajarāmaraṇa, tan katəkan tuha lara pati, nāṅ anuttara vara samyaksaṁbodhisukha, ikaṅ mokṣasukha, ikā taṅ bāhyasukha mvaṅ ādhyātmikasukha, ya ikā kamahodayan ṅa, anuṅ dinadyakən ikeṅ mahāyāna mahāmārga, yan inabhyāsa. yena yūyaṁ gamiṣyanto, apan ri səḍaṅnyan apagəha denta guməgə̄ ikeṅ kamahāyānan; bhaviṣyatha tathā⟨MS: 11v⟩gatāḥ, niyata kita tumamvaknaṅ kahyaṅbuddhān. sumākṣātkr̥ta ikaṅ kaləpasən, ikanaṅ dvayasambhāra, nāṅ jñānasambhāra, puṇyasambhāra kapaṅguha ikā denta. lāvan ikaṅ kasarvalokanamaskr̥ta kopalambha ikā denta.

(18)(19)(20)(21)(22)(23)

Dyad 7

svayambhuvo mahābhāgāḥ sarvalokasya

astināstivyatikrāntaṁ ākāśam iva nirmalam ||7||


(24)

ka: kady aṅgāniṅ ākāśa anirmalasvabhāva, alakṣaṇa, avastuka, tan kavənaṅ tinuduh, tan agə̄ṅ, tan aḍəmit, tan hirəṅ, tan putih, vyāpaka lumrā riṅ daśadeśa, maṅkana lvirnira.

(25)

Dyad 8

gambhīraṁ sarvatarkebhir apy atarkyam anāvilaṁ

sarvaprapañcarahitaṁ prapañcebhiḥ prapañcitaṁ ||8||


(26)

ka: nora mahāgambhīra lena saṅka rike saṅ hyaṅ mahāyāna mahāmārga sireki gambhīrātigambhīra: ⟨MS: 12r⟩ adaləm sakeṅ adaləm; apy atarkyaṁ: tan kavənaṅ tinarka, salah yan inuha; anāvilaṁ, tar padoṣa; sarvaprapañcarahitaṁ; tan katəkan deniṅ sarvaprapañca, mvaṅ kleśopakleśa, nāṅ: mada, dambha, lobha, moha, rajah, tamah, tan tama ikā kabeh ri sira, tuhu karikā tan >pakāvak iṅ maṅkana tahāvih, prapañcebhiḥ prapañcitaṁ, āpan ikaṅ rāga-dveṣa-moha, prapañca pinakāvaknira.

(27)(28)

Dyad 9

karmakriyāvirahitaṁ satyadvayam anāśrayaṁ

idam yānavaraṁ śreṣṭhaṁ labhiṣyatha naye sthitāḥ ||9||


(29)

ka: tan gave tan si magavai. satyadvayaṁ: tamolah makarūpa ikaṅ satyadvaya, ṅa, saṁvr̥ttisatya, paramārthasatya, anuṅ pinakarūpanira. ⟨MS: 12v⟩ anāśrayaṁ: tan pāndəlan tan samvr̥ttisatya tan paramārthasatya kahananira, idaṁ yānavaraṁ śreṣṭhaṁ, yekā sinaṅguh mahāyāna mahāmārga ṅa, manəkakən irikaṅ svargāpavarga. abhyasyata naye sthitāḥ, ya tikābhyasanta sārisāri məneṅ hələm saṅ hyaṅ mantranaya mahāyāna.

(30)

Dyad 10

mantra

oṁ! vajrodaka oṁ aḥ huṁ! iki śapathahr̥daya.

idaṁ te nārakaṁ vāri samayātikramād dahet

samayarakṣaṇāt siddhyai siddhaṁ vajrāmr̥todakaṁ ||10||


(31)(32)(33)

ka: vehana kita manahapa vajrodaka. ikaṅ vajrodaka tan vvay samānya, vvai sakeṅ naraka ikā; samayātikramo vahet, mārganiṅ duhkha kapaṅguha, bhraṣṭa ⟨MS: 13r⟩ sakulagotrabāndhava, ya tat pituhva samaya. kālanyat varavīrya rikeṅ saṅ hyaṅ vajrajñāna, samayarakṣaṇāt siddhye, kunaṅ ri səḍaṅnyat prayatna, tan paṅraparapā riṅ samaya, mārganiṅ hayu kasiddhyan kapaṅguha denta siddhaṁ bajrāmr̥todakaṁ, saṅkṣepanya: viṣāmr̥ta ikeṅ vajrodaka, vvah sahiṅga tinaṅkəp, pilih sukha pilih duhkha kapaṅguha. yat pramāda kita pamaṅguh duhkha, kunaṅ yat prayatna, avās ikaṅ sukha hayu kasiddhyan kapaṅguha usən, ṅūniveh dlāha.

(34)(35)(36)

Dyad 11

vajraṁ ghaṇṭāṁ ca mudrāṁ ca yady amaṇḍalino vadet

hased vāśraddhadāneva janaḥ saṁgaṇikāsthitaḥ ||11||


(37)(38)

ka: hayva kita umaramarahakən ika saṅ hyaṅ vajra ghaṇṭā mudrā riṅ vvaṅ adr̥ṣṭamaṇḍala, tapvan sāmayika rahasyan kubdan atah si⟨MS: 13v⟩ra, tan avaravīryākna irikaṅ vvaṅ tapvan kr̥topadeśa, hased vāśraddhavān eva, athavi guyu-guyun ta kunaṅ si tan pituhun arthanira, tan āmbəkta təmən tumarima brata bhaṭāra, hayva ta maṅkana, yāvat taṅ vvaṅ apahasa ri saṅ hyaṅ mārga, janaḥ saṅganikāsthitaḥ, avās ikaṅ vvaṅ maṅkana, kasaṅsāra sadākāla, mataṅnyan hayva tan tulus adhimukti rike saṅ hyaṅ vajrajñāna, kayatnaknātah saṅ hyaṅ samaya.

(39)(40)

Dyad 12

ayaṁ te samayo vajri vajrasattva iti smr̥taḥ

āveśaya tu tenaiva vajrajñānam anuttaram ||12||


(41)(42)

ka: saṅ hyaṅ samaya ta sira sinaṅguh bhaṭāra vajrasattva; āveśayatu tenaiva bajrajñānam anuttaraṁ, sira teki pinakahr̥dayanta maṅke, vajrajñāna ikuṅ pinaka⟨MS: 14r⟩hr̥dayanta, pahenak tāmbəkta.


Dyad 13

oṁ vajrasattvaḥ svayaṁ te ’dya cakṣūdghāṭanatatparaḥ

udghāṭayati sarvākṣo vajracakṣur anuttaram ||13||


ka: bhaṭāra śrī vajrasattva muvah hana ri matanta maṅke, cakṣūdghāṭanatatparaḥ, denira dumliṅakna panonta, mataṅnya pahabuṅah tāmbəkta, udghāṭayati sarvākṣo bajracakṣur anuttaraṁ, dəliṅakanta matanta, pahavās ta panonta ri saṅ hyaṅ maṇḍala.


Dyad 14

idaṁ ca maṇḍalam paśya śraddhāñ janaya cādunā

kule jāto ’si buddhānāṁ sarvamantrair adhiṣṭhitaḥ ||14||


(43)

ka: vulat i saṅ hyaṅ maṇḍala, śraddhāñ janayathādhunā, gavayakən taṅ śr̥ddha, hayva tan sagorava ri saṅ hyaṅ maṇḍala, kule jāto ’si buddhānāṁ, apan kita ⟨MS: 14v⟩ buddhakula maṅke, apan bhaṭāra hyaṅ buddha ṅaranta məne, sarvamantrair adhiṣṭhitaḥ, tuvi sampun kr̥tādhiṣṭhāna iki de saṅ sarvatathāgata, inajyan sinaṅaskāra rikaṅ sarvamantra.


Dyad 15

sampado ’bhimukhāḥ sarvāḥ siddhayo gatayaś ca te

pālaya samayaṁ siddhyai mantreṣūdyogavān bhava ||15||


ka: aparək tekaṅ hayu ri kita, siddhayo gatayaś ca te, samaṅkana ikaṅ kasiddhyan abhimukha ikā kabeh, agya kapaṅguha denta; pālaya samayaṁ siddhye, ləkas ta umabhyāsa saṅ hyaṅ samaya, marapvan katəmu ikaṅ kasiddhyan usən denta; mantreṣūdyogavān bhava, gavayakan taṅ utsāha ri mantra japa pūjā usən, hayva hələmhələm, yathānyan kopalambha ikaṅ kasugatin irikeṅ ihajanma ṅūniveh dlāha.


Dyad 16

mantra

⟨MS: 15r⟩ iṁ oṁ vajranetrāpahara hara paṭalaṁ hriḥ

(44)

ajñānapaṭalaṁ vatsa apanītaṁ jinais tava

śalākair vaidyarājendraiḥ yathā lokasya taimiraṁ ||16||


(45)(46)

kaliṅanya: pahenak tāmbəkta, huvus hilaṅ ikaṅ ajñānapaṭala ri hatinta, binabadan de bhaṭāra śrī vajradhara śalākair vaidyarājendraiḥ yathālokasya taimiraṁ, kady aṅgānnikanaṅ vvaṅ lara matan putikən, ramun matanya tinamvan ta ya de saṅ vedya cinələkan matanya, varas tekā matanya həniṅ, menak panonya vəkasan ri hilaṅnikaṅ kavakamalādyupadravanya, maṅkana tekiṅ ajñānapaṭalanta an hilaṅ tutas, tan paśeṣa sampun bina⟨MS: 15v⟩badan de bhaṭāra, mataṅyar pahenak ta aṅənaṅənta, hayva saṅśaya.

(47)(48)

Dyad 17

pratibimbasamā dharmā acchāḥ śuddhā hy anāvilāḥ

agrāhyānabhilāpyāś ca hetukarmasamudbhavāḥ ||17||


(49)

ka: pahavās denta umulati ikaṅ sarvadharma, tan hana pahinya lāvan māyā riṅ darpaṇa ry avakta vās ākārarūpanikanaṅ māyā riṅ darpaṇa, ndatan kavənaṅ ginaməl, apan tan hana tattvanya; maṅkana tekiṅ sarvabhāva, ṅūniveh janmamānuṣa hetukakarma dumadyakən ike, mataṅnya kadi katon mātramātra, kintu tan hana təməntəmən.


Dyad 18

evaṁ jñātvā imān dharmān nissvabhāvān anāvilān

kuru sattvārtham atulaṁ jāto ’sy urasa tāyinām ||18||


(50)(51)

ka: pahavās ta denta guməgə̄ māyopamani sarva⟨MS: 16r⟩dharma; nissvabhāvān nānāvilān, hayva ta puṅguṅ an nissvabhāva ikiṅ sarvabhāva; kuru satvārtham atulaṁ, gavayakan taṅ kaparārthan usən, jāto aurasa tāyināṁ, apan kita maṅke jinorasa ṅaranta: anak bhaṭāra hyaṅ buddha, mataṅnyan hayva ta tan sārāmbək ata riṅ kuśalakarma, mvaṅ aṅiṅkiṅ parārtha.

(52)

Dyad 19

vajrasattvaḥ prakr̥tyaiva acchaśuddha anāvilaḥ

hr̥di tiṣṭhati te vatsa sarvabuddhādhipaḥ svayam ||19||


(53)

ka: pahenak tāṅənaṅənta, bhaṭāra vajrasattva miṅasthūla sira ri hatinta, bhaṭāra vajrasattva ṅarannira; accāśuddha hy anāvilaḥ, śuddhasvabhāva sira, tan hana rāga, dveṣa, moha ri sira, tuvi ta pinakapradhāna saṅ sarvatathāgata sira, pinakahatinta sira maṅke, mārganiṅ puṇya⟨MS: 16v⟩jñānasambhāra kapaṅguha denta donira hana, hayva ta sandeha.

(54)

Dyad 20

adyaprabhr̥ti lokasya cakraṁ vartaya tāyināṁ

sarvatra pūrya vimalaṁ dharmaśaṅkham anuttaram ||20||


ka: məne tamvayanta cakraṁ vartaya tāyināṁ, umindərakan dharmacakra bhaṭāra śrī vajradhara rikaṅ sarvasattva; sarvatra pūrya vimalaṁ dharmaśaṅkham anuttaraṁ, kunaṅ deyanta hibəki lyābi pənuhi teki daśadig anantaparyyanta sakalalokadhātu, kapva hibəkananta dharmaśaṅkha ikā kabeh.

(55)

Dyad 21

na te ’tra vimatiḥ kāryā nirviśaṅkena cetasā

prakāśaya mahātulaṁ mantracaryānayam param ||21||


(56)(57)

ka: hayva kita vicikitsa, nirviśaṅkena cetasā, ikaṅ nissandeha atah ambəkakanta, prakāśaya mahātulaṁ ⟨MS: 17r⟩ mantracāryanayam paraṁ, at pintonakna ike, saṅ hyaṅ mantranaya mahāyāna.

(58)

Dyad 22

evaṁ kr̥tajño buddhānāṁ upakārīti gīyate

te ca vajradharāḥ sarve rakṣanti tava sarvaśaḥ ||22||


ka: apan ikaṅ vvaṅ kadi kita huvus kr̥tasaṅskāra ri bhaṭāra, gumave pūjā viśeṣa ri bhaṭāra hyaṅ buddha, upakārīti gīyate, ya ikā sinaṅgah sampun maveh upakāra ri bhaṭāra ṅaran ikaṅ vvaṅ maṅkana, te ca vajradharāḥ sarve rakṣanti tava sarvaśaḥ, kopakāran pva sira denta, rəṇa tāmbəknira, ya ta mataṅnya yatna rumakṣa kita ri rahina vəṅi, sakvanta saparanta sagaventa, at kita kinayatnakən denira ri vruhnira an sampun kopakāran ⟨MS: 17v⟩ denta, ya mataṅnya hayva vicikitsa, apan hana bhaṭāra śrī vajrasattva pinakātmarakṣanta sira.

(59)(60)(61)

Dyad 23

nāsti kiñcid akartavyaṁ prajñopāyena cetasā

nirviśaṅkaḥ sadā bhūtvā prabhuṅkṣva kāmapañcakam ||23||


ka: nora gavai anuṅ tan kavənaṅa gavayanta yadyapin tribhuvanaduṣkara lviraniṅ karma, tan kavənaṅa ginave de saṅ hana riṅ svarga, manuṣya, pātāla, ikān maṅkana atiduṣkaranikaṅ karma kavənaṅ ikā ya ginave denta; prajñopāyena cetasā, ndan ikaṅ prajñā atah ambəkakənanta, nirviśaṅkaḥ sadā bhūtvā, lāvan tan kahilaṅana atah kita irika nissandehacitta sadākāla; prabhuṅkṣva kāmapañcakam, paribhogan taṅ pañca kāmaguṇa denta, salvirniṅ kavi⟨MS: 18r⟩ṣayan hayva pinilihan paribhogan kabeh denta, āpan donnikadi kita sādhaka, ndan hayvatah tan pakāmbək ika nissaṅśaya.

(62)(63)(64)(65)(66)

Dyad 24

yathā hi vinayaṁ pānti bodhisattvāś svabhāvataḥ

tathā hi sarvasattvārthaṁ kuryād rāgādibhiś śuciḥ ||24||


(67)(68)(69)

ka: kady aṅgān bhaṭāra sikasa bodhisattva mahāsattvā hanāmbək təmən sira guməgə̄ i saṅ hyaṅ mantranaya. ambək təmən ṅaranya: kumiṅkiṅa kaparārthān, tan kalepanāna deniṅ kleśa, tan kapalitana deniṅ rāga dveṣa moha.

(70)(71)(72)

Dyad 25

ye cānye samayadviṣṭāḥ samayabhraṣṭā ye janāḥ
