Digital Diplomatic Edition of an Old Javanese text in manuscript Raflles Java 1 (Royal Asiatic Society, London) or ‘Directions for winning success in war by attending to certain positions & superstitions’

edited by Arlo Griffiths & Aditia Gunawan

  • Witness
  • Metadata

Witness

  • MS: Royal Asiatic Society, London, Manuscripts, Raffles Java 1
    • Physical Description: Papers manuscript, containing multiple texts. Copied at the behest of Stamfor Raflles before 1816.
    • Hand Description: A1H1Main hand of the manuscript, black ink, an apparently rather successful eye-copy of Old West Javanese Quadratic Script. The layar is noted in Indonesian mode.
    • History: Acquired by Stamford Raffles from an as yet undetermined source.
