The Kalpabuddha

Digital Critical Edition and Translation of an Old Javanese Buddhist Text

edited by Arlo Griffiths

Current Version: draft, 2024-08-31Z
Still in progress – do not quote without permission.
  • Witnesses
  • Metadata

List of Witnesses

  • EdB:
  • EdB2: This translation/re-edition does not reproduce the footnotes with variant readings for the texts edited in the 1929 version.
  • ms876: A manuscript consulted by Bosch in Batavia and referred to by him as kropak 876, where the present text was found on folio 12v ff.
  • CB113: Leiden University Library CB 113: a transcript in Balinese script made on behalf of C.C. Berg in 1929 from a lontar in the collection of Jlantik. The present text is found on folios 22r through 25r of the original lontar as indicated in the transcript. Photographed by Andrea Acri in 2011.
  • LOr9456: Leiden University Library Or 9456: a roman transcript from Kirtya IIIb.776/24. Photographed by Andrea Acri in 2011. The present text is found on pages 1–2.
