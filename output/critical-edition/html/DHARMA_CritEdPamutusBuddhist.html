List of Witnesses

  • fakeID: University Library (UB), Leiden, Lontar, Or. 5068
    • Physical Description: Palm-leaf manuscript. 89 leaves in Kuṭilā script. Apparently written in 1152 A.C.
    • History: In June 1934, Sāṅkṛtyāyana found this manuscript in the monastery of Kun-de-ling (Lhasa).
