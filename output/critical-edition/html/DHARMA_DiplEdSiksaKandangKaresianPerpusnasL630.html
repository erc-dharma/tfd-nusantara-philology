Witness

    Manuscripts kept in National Library of Indonesia
  • Jakarta, Perpustakaan Nasional Republik Indonesia, Lontar, L. 630
    • Content:
      • Saṅ Hyaṅ Siksa Kandaṅ Karesian by Anonymous
    • Physical Description: Gebang manuscript. 29 leaves in Old Western Javanese quadratic script. Apparently written in 1518 CE.
    • History: The manuscript describes the daily life of the Sundanese people as early as the 16th century and is considered a kind of Old Sundanese encyclopedia
