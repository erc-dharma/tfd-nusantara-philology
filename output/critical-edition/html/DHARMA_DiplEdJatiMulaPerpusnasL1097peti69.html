    • Physical Description: Palm-leaf (gebang) manuscript. 55 leaves in Western Old Javanese script with ink. Apparently written circa 1400 c.
    • History: This manuscript originates from Kabuyutan Koleang in Jasinga, Bogor, Indonesia. It was acquired by the Bataviaasch Genootschap van Kunsten en Wetenschappen (BGKW), along with other manuscripts from Jasinga. Subsequently, this collection was housed in the National Museum of Indonesia before being moved to the National Library of Indonesia, where it remains today.
