Witness

  • : Perpustakaan Nasional Republik Indonesia, Lontar, L. 633
    • Physical Description: This manuscript consists of 41 \textit{gebang} leaves, each measuring 22.6 × 4 cm, stored in a red wooden box, four lines per folio. There is no indication when the manuscript was copied, and the scribe only mentions that the writing was completed in the tenth month, in a place called Sunya. The title on the label is “Serat Sewaka”. A copy in Roman script is available in Perpusnas no. 264 Peti 89. The folios are numbered 1–40 by the copyist, but there are some numbering errors: 0 instead of 10 (fol. 11v), 6 instead of 16 (fol. 17v), 10 instead of 20, 30 instead of 40. In addition, among these 40 folio numbers, there is no folio 33; after folio 16 the manuscript jumps to 19, back to number 17, then 18. However, the text is coherent in its arrangement. This order indicates that the folio numbering was added after the entire text was completed.
    • Hand Description: The use of \textit{panglayar} in ms. B is limited to the Indonesian pattern.The spelling of \textit{damma} (for \textit{dharma}) is rather remarkable as it is found 26x of occurrences.item The use of consonant+\textit{pasangan y}+vowel (CyV) to be read as CVy is not found in this manuscript.
    • History: The manuscript is from Tarogong, Garut (ROD 1914: 71).
